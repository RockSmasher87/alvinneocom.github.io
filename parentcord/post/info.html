Info on sending mail:

Want to send mail?
Please PM ararouge on Discord or Minecraft if you'd like to ship anything to anyone at any time. Mail can be sent to and from anywhere with the Curtigrad Post.
Mail types:
Classified - $10 from Curtigrad to anywhere, $15 from anywhere to Curtigrad - Mail information will not be available to anyone after sending and will have priority perks. The existence of the mail will be kept secret.
Priority - $5 from Curtigrad to anywhere, $10 from anywhere to Curtigrad - Mail will be sent ASAP with extra priority.
Economy - $3 from Curtigrad to anywhere, $5 from anywhere to Curtigrad - Mail will be sent when possible to the recipient.
Discount - Free from Curtigrad to anywhere, not available outside of Curtigrad - Mail will be sent after all other mail is handled to the recipient.

All proceeds will go towards the town bank.
Deposit your mail in the row asked in the mail office once you are given a row number if you are in Curtigrad.
If you are sending TO Curtigrad, wait for the mailman to get to your locaation and give them the package to deliver.
For Discount, Economy, and Priority mail, anyone can check the status of the mail on the website.
